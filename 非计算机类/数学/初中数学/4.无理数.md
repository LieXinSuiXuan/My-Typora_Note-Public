# 无理数





$$
\sqrt{2}\text{，}\sqrt{3}\text{，}\pi \ \text{这种无限不循环小数叫做无理数}
$$



$$
\text{实数}\left\{ \begin{array}{l}
	\text{有理数}\\
	\\
	\text{无理数}\\
\end{array} \right.
$$
